Founder on the DIOR

Christian Ernest Dior was a French fashion designer and founder of one of the world's top fashion houses such as Christian Dior.

He was born on 21 January, 1905 in France and died from sudden heart attack on 24 October, 1957 in Italy.

He founded the house of Christian Dior on December 16, 1946 at 30 Avenue Montaigne Paris, backed by Marcel Boussac, a cotton-fabric magnate.

Officially, the house of Dior considers 1947 to be the year of conception as that is when Dior showed his first collection.

The Dior brand is synonymous with luxury, elegance, and taste. Its history and influence on the world of fashion are undeniable. With designers like Maria Grazia Chiuri, it continues to bring new trends and collections that celebrate beauty and charm.

The DIOR is multinational luxury goods company now is controlled and chaired by Bernard Arnault.
