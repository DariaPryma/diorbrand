Accessories

This section will guid you on five diferent types of accessories such as bags, bracelets, rings, necklaces and sunglasses.

Bags

Lady Dior

Lady Dior bag epitomizes vision of beauty. Sleek and refined, the timeless creation is crafted in black lambskin with stitching, creating quilted texture. Pale gold metal D.I.O.R. charms offer elegant appeal. Featuring a thin, removable leather shoulder strap, the bag can be carried by hand or worn crossbody.

Saddle

Saddle bag crafted in black grained calfskin, the design features a flap with a D stirrup strap, magnetic pull and an antique gold metal CD signature on sides of handle. Equipped with a thin removable strap,bag may be carried by hand, worn over the shoulder or crossbody.

Book Tote

Book Tote designed to hold daily essentials, the style is fully embroidered with the ecru and blue Dior Oblique motif. Adorned with the Christian Dior Paris signature on the front, the bag exemplifies the House's signature savoir-faire, may be carried by hand or worn over the shoulder.

Jewellery

GEM DIOR Bracelet

Bracelet is inspired by fabric samples pinned to the walls of Christian Dior ateliers and reinterpreted in the form of mineral strata, the GEM DIOR collection is distinguished by its graphic lines. Adorned with white gold and diamonds, the ultracontemporary GEM DIOR styles offer unique twist to women's outfit.

Mimirose Necklace

Necklace is a collection by Victoire de Castellane, Mimirose, celebrates the 20th anniversary of Dior Fine Jewelry. It draws inspiration from both Mimioui, with its finesse and precious stones, and Rose des Vents, with its delicate bead setting that adorns each stone.

Bois de Rose Ring

Ring is the Bois de Rose Collection pays homage to Christian Dior's favorite flower, the rose, by reinterpreting its stem in a stunning abstraction. Punctuated with golden or diamond thorns, it wraps delicately around the finger, wrist or ear as a symbol of attachment.

Sunglasses

MissDior S1I

MissDior S1I sunglasses expand the namesake line with an elegantly modern rectangular shape. The black acetate frame is accented by wide temples showcasing the Miss Dior signature in gold-finish metal. Featuring gray lenses, the eye-catching sunglasses will effortlessly complete any look.

Lady 95.22 M1I

Lady 95.22 M1I black acetate sunglasses with gray lenses debut a new House line showcasing an ultra-modern aesthetic. The shape is embellished with the iconic Cannage motif on the temples, the hinge in gold metal. Sunglasses combine modernity with sportswear elegance.

DiorClub M1U

DiorClub M1U sunglasses are a rectangular-shaped with a sportswear appeal. The silver mirrored lenses in a blue Dior Oblique motif, the design features spoilers that lend a modern touch. The navy blue frame contrasts with a white line detail and DIOR logo.
